Our promise

The Guarantee

The Dream State Limited · Company No. 16801178 · Last updated: 2026

We don't sell money-back theatre. We guarantee the things that are actually in our control — and we keep them.

What we guarantee

  • The methodology. Every build runs through our full customer-journey engineering system — research, diagnosis, journey engineering — not intuition dressed up as taste.
  • The standard. Every build is checked against our 17-point Build Standard before handover.
  • The timeline. We commit to the delivery window stated in your tier and Scope of Work, and we hold ourselves to it.
  • The support. You get the structured communication and support window set out in your engagement — no ghosting.
  • Honesty about fit. If we don't think we're the right fit for you, we'll say so before you spend.

What we don't guarantee

We do not guarantee a specific revenue figure, conversion rate, ROAS or ranking. Anyone who promises you a number is guessing. Commercial results depend on factors beyond any single website — your product, pricing, ad spend, market and operations among them. The results shown on this site are real and repeatedly demonstrated, but they are never sold as a promise of what your store will do.

If we break a promise

If any of the guarantees above is not met because of something within our control, that is a real failure and we will put it right — by completing the work to standard, correcting the issue, or agreeing a fair resolution with you directly. This guarantee sits alongside your statutory rights and the terms of your Scope of Work; it does not replace them, and nothing here creates a promise of a financial outcome.

This guarantee is provided in good faith for launch and forms part of our wider Terms of Service. See also our Refunds Policy and Privacy Policy.
