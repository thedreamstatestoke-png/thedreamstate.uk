Refunds & Cancellation Policy

The Dream State Limited · Company No. 16801178 · Last updated: 2026

Working draft. This policy is a clear, good-faith starting point for launch and will be replaced by a solicitor-reviewed version. The terms that apply to your engagement are those in your signed Scope of Work, which mirror this policy.

Conversion Audit

The Audit is a fixed-scope diagnostic delivered as a written report and an accompanying consultation call. Because the deliverable and call are the product, the Audit is non-refundable once the report has been delivered or the call has taken place. If we are unable to deliver the Audit, you receive a full refund.

Shopify Builds — 48-hour cancellation window

  • Within 48 hours of your deposit clearing: you may cancel for a refund of your deposit less a £200 administration fee, which covers contracting, scheduling and capacity reserved for your project.
  • After 48 hours: research and preparatory work begins, the deposit pays for the work being performed, and it is non-refundable. If you cancel after this point, the project stops cleanly but amounts paid are not recoverable.
  • The 48-hour window is genuine — we do not begin chargeable research during it.

Wrong-fit exit

If, before substantial delivery, we identify that the engagement is not the right fit, we may initiate a wrong-fit exit and refund 50% of the deposit. This is initiated by us and is designed to prevent either side being trapped in an engagement that won't land.

Conversion Retainer

  • Retainers are rolling monthly and require 30 days' written notice to cancel.
  • Notice takes effect from your next billing date; the current paid month completes in full.
  • We do not pro-rate or refund part-months.

How to cancel

All cancellations must be requested in writing by email to thedreamstate.sje@gmail.com. We will confirm receipt and the effect of your cancellation.

Your statutory rights

Nothing in this policy affects any rights you have under applicable law. Where a service is supplied to you as a consumer, any cancellation rights that apply by law are honoured in addition to the terms above.
