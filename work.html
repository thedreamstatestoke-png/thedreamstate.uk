From the Audits

Leaks we find again and again.

A sample of anonymised diagnostic findings — the patterns costing fashion brands real money every week.

PDP architecture

Product pages leading with specs, not desire. No like-want-need sequence. Buyers leave before the "want" ever lands.

Hero coherence

The ad promises one thing, the landing hero says another. The mismatch breaks trust in the first two seconds.

Mobile optimisation

Desktop-first builds on stores where 70%+ of traffic is mobile. The money is leaking on the screen most buyers use.

Popup stacking

Three popups firing on entry. Friction dressed up as "optimisation," quietly killing the journey.

Social proof placement

Reviews buried below the fold, or absent at the exact moment of doubt. Proof in the wrong place is no proof at all.

Klarna / Clearpay readiness

Pay-later options missing or hidden — for a buyer who decides on affordability before they decide on the product.
